Dr. K.K. Khandelwal

Specialization: Cardiology

Dr. K.K. Khandelwal is a renowned cardiologist with over 20 years of experience in diagnosing and treating heart conditions. He is well-versed in managing complex cardiovascular diseases and has a keen interest in preventive cardiology. Dr. Khandelwal has contributed to numerous research papers and is a member of various national and international cardiology associations.

Education

  • MBBS from All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi
  • MD in Internal Medicine from AIIMS, New Delhi
  • DM in Cardiology from AIIMS, New Delhi

Experience

  • Senior Consultant, Cardiology, Apollo Hospitals, New Delhi
  • Associate Professor of Cardiology, AIIMS, New Delhi
  • Visiting Faculty, American College of Cardiology

Awards & Recognitions

  • Padma Shri Award, 2015
  • Best Cardiologist Award by Indian Medical Association, 2018
  • Excellence in Cardiology by HealthCare India, 2021
