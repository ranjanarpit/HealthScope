Maintain a Healthy Weight

Losing 5-10% of Body Weight: If you are overweight, losing even a small percentage of your body weight can have a significant impact on your health. It can lower blood sugar levels, reduce blood pressure, and improve cholesterol levels.

Healthy Weight Loss: Aim for a gradual weight loss of 1-2 pounds per week through a combination of healthy eating and regular physical activity. Rapid weight loss is often unsustainable and can lead to muscle loss and nutritional deficiencies.

Long-Term Maintenance: Once you've achieved your weight loss goals, focus on maintaining that weight by continuing healthy habits like balanced eating and regular physical activity.
