Dr. R.P. Singh

Specialization: Orthopedics

Dr. R.P. Singh is a leading orthopedic surgeon with over 18 years of experience in treating musculoskeletal disorders. His expertise includes complex joint replacements and spinal surgeries. Dr. Singh is committed to advancing orthopedic techniques and has published extensively in medical journals. He is an active member of various orthopedic associations globally.

Education

  • MBBS from King George's Medical University, Lucknow
  • MS in Orthopedics from Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER), Chandigarh
  • Fellowship in Joint Replacement Surgery from Royal College of Surgeons, UK

Experience

  • Chief Orthopedic Surgeon, Medanta Hospital, Gurgaon
  • Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon, Apollo Hospitals, Hyderabad
  • Visiting Professor, Harvard Medical School

Awards & Recognitions

  • Lifetime Achievement Award in Orthopedics, 2016
  • Best Orthopedic Surgeon Award by Indian Orthopedic Association, 2019
  • Global Excellence in Orthopedics Award, 2023
